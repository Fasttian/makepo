about CATIK LIGHTING

CATIK LIGHTING CO., LTD. Ltd is a subsidiary of the state-owned enterprise AVICINTERNATIONAL.

AVIC SHENZHEN TRADING CORPORATION, Ltd is a subsidiary of the state-owned enterprise AVICINTERNATIONAL. The corporation has long specialized in the import and export business in LED lighting and other electrical products. With the abundant financial resources, professional services, good reputation, and a high-quality professional team, the corporation has kept a good record in the scale of the annual import-export business for over $100, 000, 000 during the past ten years.

The corporation specializes in designing, manufacturing, and selling varieties of lighting products, mainly including LED light source products, commercial lighting as well as outdoor lighting products.

corporation keeps the faith that reputation comes first and quality comes first. We strive to develop the quality and cost performance to the extreme.

Our technology team will make changes and improvements in the design and function of the product so as to meet the different demands from different regions and countries. We also make the best-selling products through our own independent research and development.

Customers from both home and abroad are warmly welcome! We believe that our sincerity and hospitality will bring you light-like brilliance and warmth!
